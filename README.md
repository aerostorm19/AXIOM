# AXIOM

> **Behavioral intelligence for human relationships.**

AXIOM transforms your conversation history into measurable behavioral signals, helping you understand relationships through observed interaction patterns instead of memory or intuition.

Rather than interpreting **what people say**, AXIOM analyzes **how communication actually happens**—who initiates, how engagement changes over time, how reciprocal conversations are, and whether relationships are strengthening or fading.

---

## Why?

Humans are remarkably poor at evaluating relationships objectively.

We rely on memory, emotions, and recent experiences to judge who matters in our lives. Those signals are inherently biased.

- A friend who supported you months ago may still *feel* close despite rarely engaging today.
- Someone who replies warmly but never initiates conversations can appear emotionally invested while contributing little to the relationship.
- Recent interactions often outweigh months or years of consistent behavioral patterns.

In practice, we evaluate relationships using intuition instead of evidence.

---

## The Missing Signal

Every digital relationship leaves behind a behavioral footprint.

WhatsApp chats, iMessage conversations, Telegram messages, Discord DMs, Slack threads, emails—these are not just conversations. They are longitudinal records of interaction.

Within them are measurable signals such as:

- Initiation frequency
- Response latency
- Reciprocity
- Conversation balance
- Engagement decay
- Communication consistency
- Relationship momentum

Unlike subjective perception, these signals are derived directly from observed behavior.

AXIOM exists to extract that information.

---

## What AXIOM Does

AXIOM converts communication history into quantitative relationship metrics.

Instead of asking:

> *"Does this person value this relationship?"*

it asks questions that can be measured:

- Who starts conversations?
- Who keeps them alive?
- Is effort becoming more or less balanced?
- Has engagement increased or declined over time?
- Which relationships are genuinely reciprocal?
- Which exist primarily through one-sided effort?

The goal is **measurement, not judgment**.

---

## Design Principles

- **Behavior over assumptions** — Analyze observable interaction patterns instead of interpreting intent.
- **Objective metrics** — Every score should be reproducible from communication data.
- **Privacy first** — Conversation analysis runs locally; your data stays on your device.
- **Research-driven** — Metrics are inspired by behavioral science, network analysis, and human-computer interaction.
- **Explainable results** — Every insight is backed by measurable evidence, not black-box predictions.

---

## Vision

Relationships are among the most important aspects of human life, yet we evaluate them using intuition alone.

AXIOM aims to provide an evidence-based layer for understanding interpersonal dynamics by converting communication behavior into transparent, interpretable metrics.

Not to tell people **who they should care about**— but to help them see **what the data already shows**.
